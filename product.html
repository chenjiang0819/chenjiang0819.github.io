One from Martin's "Golden Era" series. A range of guitars that celebrate the design, quality, and timbers that all came together in the Pre World War 2 years. Style 18, So It has a Honduran Mahogany Back, Sides and Neck, "Adirondack" Spruce Top (AKA Red or Eastern Spruce) without much runout, as in the grain lines aren't too far apart from each other. Ebony Fretboard and Bridge, Rosewood Headstock overlay. Waverley vintage style Tuners, Fossilised Walrus Ivory Nut, Bridge Saddle and Pins complete the picture. a treat to play and it sounds like a classic old Blues record.
